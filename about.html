Subnautica

Subnautica is an underwater survival game on planet 4546B where your goal is to escape and return back home. The game begins with the Aurora, your spaceship, crash landing in the ocean, but you're able to escape on lifepod #5, one of 9 lifepods to succesfully land in the ocean. However, you will soon find that you're the only survivor. In order to escape you will need to gather resources in dangerous biomes, face vicious alien fauna, and collect data in abandoned lifepods, and alien precursor bases to construct a device to set you free. But before you escape, you'll need to cure and eradicate the planet's dissease.

Unkown Worlds Entertainment

Subnautica was launched as early access on December 16, 2014 by Unkown Worlds Entertainment, and remained that way for roughly 3 years until it was fully released on January 23, 2018. Unnkown worlds is a small independant video game development company based in San Francisco with only 33 employes.
