Fabricator

The fabricator is the crafting table table of subnautica, it makes nearly everything you need in the game. With the fabricator, you can make: resources, sustenance, personal items, and deployables. Some of the most essential items needed to craft with the fabricator are water, food, tools, and a vehichle bay. Some of the most common resources used to craft items are titanium, copper, silver, gold, and more.
