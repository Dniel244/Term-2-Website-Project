Subnautica

Subnautica is an underwater survival game on planet 4546B where your goal is to escape and return back home. The game begins with the Aurora, your spaceship, crash landing in the ocean, but you're able to escape on lifepod #5, one of 9 lifepods to succesfully land in the ocean. However, you will soon find that you're the only survivor. In order to escape you will need to gather resources in dangerous biomes, face vicious alien fauna, and collect data in abandoned lifepods, and alien precursor bases to construct a device to set you free. But before you escape, you'll need to cure and eradicate the planet's dissease. check out the Subnautica wiki here.

Penglin:

The DLC (downloadable content) for subnautica is Subnautica: Below Zero, which takes place on a different part of the same planet, but with different characters. In Below Zero you are'nt trying to escape, rather you're researching the Zero sector of the planet, but things don't go to smoothly. Below Zero is currently in early access and being updated with tons of cool features the original game doesn't have.There is also a Below Zero wiki.
