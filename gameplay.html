Gameplay

Overall, I would say the gameplay is fairly smooth with the highest graphics. The graphics of subnautica are phenomenal, especially the water effect. You will have lots of game time your first time through, as you are trying to figure out the games mechanics and the location of important biomes or ruins. However, the second time through you will go through the game much quicker, but you will also see and make things you didn't the first time through.
