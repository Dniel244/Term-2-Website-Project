Subnautica

Subnautica is an underwater survival game on planet 4546B where your goal is to escape and return back home. The game begins with the Aurora, the spaceship you are aboard, crash landing in the ocean, but you're able to escape on lifepod #5, one of 9 lifepods to succesfully land in the ocean. However, you will soon find that you're the only survivor. In order to escape you will need to gather resources in dangerous biomes, face vicious alien fauna, and collect data in abandoned lifepods, and alien precursor bases to construct a device to set you free. But before you escape, you'll need to cure and eradicate the planet's dissease.

Personal Experience

My personal experience with Subnautica has been phenomenal. Subnautica is by far my favourite survival game ever played, with the amazing graphics mysterious music, and smooth gameplay, it realy makes you feel like you yourself are on this alien planet. Sometimes, while playing subnautica, I will always remember my first encounter with a reaper leviathan as being one of the most exhilarating experiences of my life. All in all, I love this game and highly recomend it to anybody who's interested in playing it.

Unnkown worlds is a small, independant, video game development company based in San Francisco with only 33 employes. Unkown Worlds launched Subnautica on December 16, 2014 as an early access game, and it remained early access for roughly 3 years until it was fully released on January 23, 2018.

DLC

The DLC (downloadable content) for Subnautica is Subnautica: Below Zero, which takes place on a different part of the same planet, but with different characters. In Below Zero you are'nt trying to escape, rather you're researching the Zero sector of the planet, but things don't go to smoothly. Below Zero is currently in early access and being updated with tons of cool features the original game doesn't have. One of the new features is the hoverbike
